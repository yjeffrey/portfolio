What

This website is an e-portfolio meant to showcase what I am capable of creating, and also demo anything mildly interesting I have created over the years.

It is hosted by Heroku (on one dyno), written in node.js, and uses AngularJS as a front-end framework. This site is optimally viewed in Chrome. Firefox and Safari would also work well.

This website contains ports of projects I have completed or even simple scripts I have written in Java, C++, Matlab, etc... These pieces of code have been converted into JavaScript and are here for your hopefully viewing pleasure.

Notable frameworks used

By the server:

  • Express.js

By the client:

  • AngularJS
  • Bootstrap
  • D3/C3
  • JQuery
  • RequireJS
